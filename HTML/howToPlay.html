HOW TO PLAY DUCK DECKERS

1. Make a room code for your opponent to join with
2. The player who created the room goes first.
3. Take turns placing ducks to attack the enemy.
4. To play a duck, make sure you have enough Emeralds, then click the card in your hand and click a tile on your side
5. To attack with a duck, click a duck in play and hit SPACE. Then click on your target
6. You must defeat all the other ducks on the opponent's side in order to attack the player.
7. At the start of your turn, you will gain Emeralds. Emeralds do not carry over, so spend them!
8. Who ever drops their opponent's health to zero first wins!
